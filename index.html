Comfortability at its peak

Lavender Hotel is located at such a serene geographical place in Lagos, the western part of Nigeria. All 24 hours room are different as far as space, decor and view are concerned. However, they are all well funished with state of the art to beat the taste of our visitors.

In addition to the comfortability of our rooms, we have top-notch hotel amenities such as well treated swimming polls, state of the art gym/fitness center, all kinds of sport halls, a free shuttle service and lot more. Whether you have a hotel, resort, lodge, or a vacation rental, we strive to be your partner in creating memorable guest experiences.

A Taste of Nature

Lavender has everything you could possibly wish for in a tropical beach resort, and much more. There’s something to suit everyone, whether you’re visiting for business or pleasure, relaxation or stimulation. Meet the dolphins in our private lagoon, relax by the white sand beach, learn to surf or scuba, and take an exercise class overlooking the ocean.

The Food You Crave

At Lavender there is nothing more important to us than making sure visitors enjoy their stay. That’s why our kitchen offers excellent cuisine every single day of the week, while weekend diners are in for a real treat as our restaurant hosts special banquets, traditional Alpine entertainment evenings and even a themed Greek night!
